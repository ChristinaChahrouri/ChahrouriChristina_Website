Hour To Spare is a cozy narrative cooking game that blends
the comforting aesthetics of life-simulation games with the
emotional tension of decision-based storytelling. Inspired by
games such as The Sims 4, Animal Crossing: New Horizons,
and The Walking Dead, the game combines soft domestic
environments, interactive choices, and time-management
mechanics to create an experience that feels both warm and
emotionally engaging.


The visual identity centers around a muted pastel color
palette featuring soft blues, warm creams, dusty pinks, and
earthy browns. These colors establish a nostalgic and
comforting atmosphere reminiscent of cozy home interiors
and everyday routines. Painterly textures and blurred
imagery further enhance the dreamlike quality of the game,
creating a handcrafted aesthetic that feels intimate and
approachable.


At its core, Hour To Spare explores the balance between
comfort and pressure through ordinary domestic tasks.
Players navigate cooking challenges, timed decisions, and
distractions that influence gameplay outcomes. While the
environments feel safe and inviting, moments of urgency
and consequence introduce emotional tension, reflecting the
realities of time management and daily responsibility.
