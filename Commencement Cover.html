The 2023 Evergreen Valley College
Commencement design presents a visual
identity centered on achievement, growth,
and prestige.


The brand aesthetic combines deep
evergreen tones with metallic gold
accents to create a sophisticated and
celebratory atmosphere that reflects
academic success and institutional pride.

The use of green symbolizes growth,
renewal, and opportunity,


Typography plays an important role in
balancing professionalism with elegance.
Bold sans-serif typefaces establish clarity,
confidence, and modernity, while the serif
italic typography introduces sophistication
and ceremonial tradition.


Overall, the design system communicates
a refined and memorable brand identity
that celebrates graduates while
maintaining Evergreen Valley College’s
professional and welcoming image.
